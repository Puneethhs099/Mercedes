About us

Mercedes-Benz commonly referred to as Mercedes and sometimes as Benz, is a German luxury and commercial vehicle automotive brand established in 1926. Mercedes-Benz AG (a Mercedes-Benz Group subsidiary established in 2019) is headquartered in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. Mercedes-Benz AG produces consumer luxury vehicles and commercial vehicles badged as Mercedes-Benz. From November 2019 onwards, Mercedes-Benz-badged heavy commercial vehicles (trucks and buses) are managed by Daimler Truck, a former part of the Mercedes-Benz Group turned into an independent company in late 2021. In 2018, Mercedes-Benz was the largest brand of premium vehicles in the world, having sold 2.31 million passenger cars.

Ownership

The Mercedes-Benz Group AG is owned by European, U.S. and other international investors. More than one billion shares (December 31, 2021) are circulating.

Brands

Mercedes-Benz, Mercedes-AMG, Mercedes-Maybach, Mercedes me, Mercedes-EQ, Mercedes-Benz Bank, Mercedes-Benz Financial Services, Athlon.

Global stock

The stock is listed on the stock exchanges in Frankfurt and Stuttgart.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I obtain the radio code for my vehicle?

    To obtain your MY 1990 -2002 radio code you can: 1. Visit your local authorized Mercedes-Benz dealer. 2. Contact our Customer Assistance Center at 1-800-FOR-MERCEDES (1-800-367-6372) with your Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)

  • Can I purchase extended warranty from Mercedes-Benz?

    The Mercedes-Benz Extended Limited Warranty may be purchased through any authorized Mercedes-Benz dealership (restrictions and limitations apply).

  • Can I purchase extended coverage for my Certified Pre-Owned vehicle?

    Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) warranty coverage is available at the time of vehicle purchase. Additional coverage while within the CPO warranty period may also be available. Please see the Sales Department at the authorized Mercedes-Benz dealership to verify vehicle eligibility and pricing.
